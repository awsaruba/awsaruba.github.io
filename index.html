What does SEO stand for?

Search Engine Optimization is all about getting your website higher up in the search results on search engines such as Google, Bing, and Yandex. The higher up the results you are the more traffic (and customers) you‚Äôre likely to get.

How long does SEO take?

The answer to this question depends on the context of the question; whether you mean, how long it takes to see results, or how many hours it takes to do optimize a website. If you mean how long does it take to see results of an SEO campaign, then the answer is, nobody knows.

Search engines crawl websites at their own rate. It could be a day, or a few months to achieve positive results. There is no way of knowing when your website will be "crawled" because it is a random process.

If you are asking how long it takes, that depends on the project, and the type of website. In our experience, the initial setup takes much longer than regular SEO maintenance; usually between 5-10 hours, depending on the website, and what's being done.
